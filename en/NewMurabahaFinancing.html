Murabaha Financing

Murabaha Financing

Under a Murabaha agreement, ITFC purchases commodities directly from a supplier and sells them to a beneficiary with a deferred payment arrangement. The difference between the purchase price and the sale price is a reasonable markup added to the purchase price.

  • Import Finance
  • Pre-export Finance
  • Buyer’s Credit
Import Finance

We extend import financing by purchasing goods from international suppliers in advance and selling them to buyers in Member Countries at cost with a pre-agreed markup. The benefits to importers include:

  • Enhanced management of working capital
  • Ability to source raw materials and supplies as needed
  • Financing linked to the natural business cycle
  • Seamless integration with existing trading activities
Pre-export Finance

We finance the purchase of raw materials or other goods from local or international suppliers to meet orders from international buyers in Member Countries. The benefits to exporters include:

  • Enhanced management of working capital
  • Financing linked to the natural business cycle
  • Increased order capacity without cash flow impact
  • Flexible payment terms
Buyer’s Credit

We provide exporters with 100% payment for shipment exported under Import Financing extended to an international buyer. The benefits include:

  • Enhanced management of working capital, cash flow and liquidity
  • Mitigation of non-payment/credit risk
  • Facilitation of pricing negotiations
