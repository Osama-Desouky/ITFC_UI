The West Africa SME Program with Coris Bank International and the Organisation Internationale de la Francophonie (OIF)

A twin capacity building program for banks and SMEs that aims to improve SMEs access to Trade Lines of Finance and to accelerate disbursement of trade finance to SMEs.

The Global Trade Finance elearning Program with the ICC Academy and the EIF

partnership to develop and disseminate Islamic trade finance courses through the ICC LMS platform. The course is made available as an elective to ITFC banks as part of the Trade Finance E-Learning Program and to other ICC certificate candidates.

The Project for Harmonization of Standards for Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices with ARSO, Afreximbank, BADEA and AATB

A partnership aimed at promoting the quality and safety of medicines and medical devices imported or produced on the continent.

The Impact of the AfCFTA on selected OIC countries with the Statistical, Economic and Social Research and Training Centre for Islamic Countries (SESRIC)

A Trilogy to project the impacts of the AfCFTA on the trade flows, infrastructure development and landlocked countries in the OIC. will help derive policy and practical recommendations as of today to achieve this result
