Financial Institution

Financial Institution

ITFC’s mission is being a catalyst for trade development among OIC Member Countries and keep leading as the number one provider of trade solutions for their development needs, as such A core component of this mission is to mobilize capital from a range of sources of funds to our markets and our Beneficiaries. In carrying out its role, FI and syndication team core responsibilities are (1) managing the relationship with the Financial Institutions, and actively partner with commercial banks, institutional investors, Multilateral

Development Banks, funds and development finance institutions through Risk Participation from Partners. To achieve this objective, ITFC adopts several different arrangements

  • Benefits to partners
  • Benefits to beneficiaries
Benefits to partners
  • ITFC’s Preferred Creditor Status and Privileges and Immunities are shared, mitigating transfer and convertibility risk
  • ITFC’s relationship with member governments.
  • ITFC’s structuring and transaction assessment expertise.
  • ITFC’s supervision and monitoring of transaction implementation.
  • ITFC’s deal-flow and recurring transactions based on it’s agreements with governments
  • Potential introduction to new clients/geographies through participation in ITFC Syndicated Facilities
Benefits to beneficiaries
  • Meet the beneficiary large financing requirements
  • Enable clients to plan their financing needs on short and mid-term based on the Master Financing Agreements signed with ITFC.
  • Potential to achieve large financing with one point of contact with ITFC as Lead Arranger.
  • Simplified administration and documentation since ITFC is the Financiers on record.
  • Introducing sharia compliant financing arrangements to our beneficiaries.
